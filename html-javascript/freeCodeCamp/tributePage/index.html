  • 2000 - NBA champion
  • 2001 - NBA champion
  • 2002 - NBA champion
  • 2006 - Scored a career-high 81 points
  • 2009 - NBA champion
  • 2010 - NBA champion
  • 2016 - Played his last NBA season
